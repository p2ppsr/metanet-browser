Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 25, 2025

Privacy First: Metanet Explorer is designed with your privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share your personal browsing data.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Metanet Explorer is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect:

  • Your browsing history or websites you visit
  • Search queries or search history
  • Personal information such as name, email, or phone number
  • Location data or device identifiers
  • Cookies, cache, or other web data from your browsing sessions
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Any form of tracking or behavioral data

2. Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

2.1 Bookmarks

  • Bookmarks you save are stored locally on your device
  • Bookmark data includes website URLs and titles you choose to save
  • This data remains on your device and is not synchronized or backed up to external servers
  • You can delete bookmarks at any time through the app interface

2.2 App Preferences

  • User interface settings and preferences
  • Theme and display configurations
  • These settings are stored as tokens in your wallet

3. Wallet Authentication Backend

For cross-device login functionality, we utilize a secure authentication system:

3.1 Encrypted Key Storage

  • Keys are encrypted using industry-standard encryption before storage
  • We cannot decrypt or access the content of these keys
  • Keys are used for secure cross-device consistency when you authenticate with your phone number

3.2 Authentication Process

  • Authentication is performed using encrypted protocols
  • No personal browsing data is transmitted during authentication
  • Authentication tokens are temporary and automatically expire

4. Third-Party Services

When you browse the internet using Metanet Explorer:

  • Websites you visit may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices
  • We do not control or monitor these third-party websites
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of websites you visit
  • Any data shared with websites is between you and those websites directly

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we handle:

  • Encrypted storage for authentication keys
  • Secure transmission protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to backend systems

6. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Bookmarks and preferences remain on your device until you delete them
  • Authentication Keys: Encrypted keys are retained on Wallet Authentication Backend services
  • Account Deletion: You can request deletion of your authentication keys by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

Metanet Explorer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing notice through the app interface for significant changes

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information about what data we store (limited to encrypted authentication keys)
  • Request deletion of your authentication keys
  • Opt out of cross-device authentication by using the app in local-only mode
  • Export your locally stored bookmarks

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@bsvassociation.org
  • Organization: BSV Association
Summary: Metanet Explorer prioritizes your privacy by not collecting browsing data, storing bookmarks locally on your device, and only using encrypted authentication keys for secure cross-device login functionality.
